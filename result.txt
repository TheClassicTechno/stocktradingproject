TECHNICAL INDICATORS EXPLAINED


Moving Averages:
- 15-day MA: Very short-term trend indicator
- 30-day MA: Short-term trend (1 month)
- 50-day MA: Medium-term trend (widely watched by traders)
- 100-day MA: Intermediate trend
- 200-day MA: Long-term trend (most important support/resistance)

Interpretation:
- Price > MA = Bullish signal (uptrend)
- Price < MA = Bearish signal (downtrend)
- Golden Cross: 50-day crosses above 200-day (very bullish)
- Death Cross: 50-day crosses below 200-day (very bearish)

Trend Strength Score:
- 100% = Above all moving averages (strong bullish)
- 80% = Above most MAs (moderately bullish)
- 60% = Mixed positioning (neutral to bullish)
- 40% = Mixed positioning (neutral to bearish)
- 20% = Below most MAs (moderately bearish)
- 0% = Below all moving averages (strong bearish)

Momentum Score:
- Weighted calculation based on recent price changes
- Higher weight given to more recent periods
- Positive score indicates upward momentum
- Negative score indicates downward momentum

Volume:
- High volume confirms price movements
- Low volume suggests weak conviction
- Watch for volume spikes during price changes
